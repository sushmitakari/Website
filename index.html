My journey has taken me from writing code at Cognizant to leading design at IBM — with stops at Nokia, GE Healthcare, GE Digital, and Salesforce along the way. Each chapter added a new layer: research, strategy, AI, systems design.

Today I sit at the intersection of design and product management. I define concept models, influence roadmaps, run customer research, and translate complex enterprise problems into clear, adoptable experiences.

I'm especially drawn to AI-driven enterprise tools — where the stakes are high, the problems are messy, and clarity creates real value. My next step is to channel this expertise fully into a product leadership role.
